Terminology ledger:
- Netrin-1: ligand/stimulus, use Netrin-1 consistently.
- Vehicle: PBS 5-min control; use Vehicle rather than PBS except where design limitation is discussed.
- 5v0: 5 min Netrin-1 versus Vehicle.
- 15v0: 15 min Netrin-1 versus Vehicle.
- 15v5: 15 min Netrin-1 versus 5 min Netrin-1.
- DEG: differentially expressed gene.
- GSEA: gene set enrichment analysis.
- GO BP: Gene Ontology Biological Process.
- KEGG: Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes.
- NES: normalized enrichment score.
- LRT: likelihood-ratio test.
